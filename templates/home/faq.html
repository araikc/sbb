What is Solarbit?

Solarbit is an online investment platform that was developed by Solarbit Limited. It is registered in the UK (registration number #10929901) and regulated under the financial laws of the Great Britain. This information can be verified on the British government registrar’s website. What do I need to do to become a client of Solarbit Limited?

To become a client of Solarbit Limited you need to open an account. Registering an account is completely free and will take only a few minutes. After this you can officially become a member and will be able to execute your investment strategies. To learn more about this, please visit this page Get Started. Please note that by agreeing to the Terms of Use during the registration you automatically confirm that you are of legal age in your country of residence and that by using our platform you don’t violate any laws of your country of residence.

Can I have several open accounts in the system?

Each investor can legally register only one account, where he/she can create an unlimited number of deposits. Registering multiple accounts is prohibited. We will freeze any funds and block access to all such accounts. In case of suspected fraud or suspected creation of multiple accounts, Solarbit Limited reserves the right to block such accounts or prevent account access for violating investors.

How can I access the account?

If you are a registered user of Solarbit Limited, please click the “Login” button at the top of the website, enter your username and password in the appropriate fields and click "Log in" button. You will be automatically redirected to your account right away. If you have not registered yet, you should register a new account first.

What should I do immediately after accessing my account?

You can explore the system functions, set up your personal information and specify your payment system details. You can enter your e-wallet details by clicking the "Wallets" button. Please note that payment details are saved permanently, and you can change them only by contacting our customer support. This is a precaution, to protect your investment in case of unauthorized access to your accounts.

What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?

You can use password restore function - we will send you an email to your registered email with detailed instructions and a password reset/change form. If you did not receive the email, please contact us via send message to our customer support from contacts page or directly to support@solarbit.biz.

I can't log in into my account. What could cause this problem?

Sometimes these problems are caused by errors of your web browser. There are no reasons to worry, please wait a few minutes and then try to log in again. Failing that please clear your web browser cache. If the problem persists, please contact us via contacts page or send message to our customer support at support@solarbit.biz.

How secure is your website and my account data?

We have a wide range of security measures to protect your account. Our website is protected against DDoS attacks, all transferred data are SSL-encrypted. We use a licensed script for transactions together with online security certificates, you are able to see full list on our main page. Our website is located on a dedicated server.

What is the minimum/maximum amount that I can cash out?

How can I top up my account and create a deposit in the Solarbit Limited?

Can I deposit directly from my referral bonuses?

You can make a deposit from your referral bonusses. Simply log into your member account, click "Make Deposit" and invest.

After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my e-currency account?

As a rule, we’re trying to process withdrawals as fast as possible. Your funds will be available in a few hours. Maximum processing time is 72 hours.

What could be the reason for the delay more than 72 hours?

There can be several reasons for the delay: • Your e-wallet data is either missing or incorrect. • In rare cases (e.g. force majeure, technical failure, suspicion of fraud), the payment may take more than 72 hours to process.

How can I check my account balance?

What are the benefits of the referral program? How can I participate?

Our referral program gives you an opportunity to earn additional income by bringing new investors into the Solarbit system. You need to make sure that the investors you refer use your referral link, which you can find in the Referrals section of your account.

Do you have an affiliate program?

The affiliate program uses multi level referral commission which will pay 10% from each deposit of level 1 referrals, 2% from each deposit made by your level 2 referrals (people referred by your direct invitees) and 1% from each deposit made by your level 3 (people referred by your level 2 referrals).

How will I receive my referral commission?

The referral commission is accrued to your account balance instantly.

Where can I get my referral links and your banners?

You can find referral link in Referrals section. Graphic materials you can find in section Affiliate Banners section.

How do you know that a new investor was introduced by me?

The referral system works during registration and is fully automatic. If an investor signs up via your referral link, the system will treat that investor as your referral. You can see all your referred users in the Your Referrals section of your member page.

I want to bring new investors into the Solarbit system, but I have not opened a deposit yet. Can I still participate in the referral program?

Yes, you can. You don’t need an active deposit to participate in the referral program.
